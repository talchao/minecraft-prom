Changelog

Minecraft Prom 2025

April 26, 2025


Casino: !casino


  • !! NEW: Loot Boxes: Spend 2 diamonds to get a fun, random item.
  • !! NEW: Slot Machine: Bet an amount of diamonds (1, 5, or 10) for a chance to make a profit!
  • Added more plushies, pride flags, & other collectables — available via loot boxes.
  • Added Beer.

Games:


  • Connect 4: Added outlines to Connect 4 blocks for easier legibility.
  • Dodgeball: Replaced bow & arrow with a custom ball.
    • No need to charge up the dodgeball like you would a bow — simply aim and right click to throw!

General:


  • Added chairs.
  • Players now receive an on-screen message upon joining the world.
  • Court Jesters will now receive Clown Pants alongside the Court Jester Hat.
  • Added more emojis (including rock, paper, & scissors).
    • !emojis in-game for a list of all emojis.

  • !list in-game for a list of all commands.
